Fourier Visualizations

Magnitude Plot

This is the magnitude spectrum of the Fast Fourier Transform applied to the angle time series. For each angle (set of 3 joints), each time frequency has its magnitude value.
Only angles which were visible at least 50% of the time are shown.

Phase Plot

This is the phase spectrum of the Fast Fourier Transform applied to the angle time series. For each angle (set of 3 joints), each time frequency has its phase value.
Only angles which were visible at least 50% of the time are shown.
